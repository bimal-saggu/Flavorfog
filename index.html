About Us

At FlavorFog Kitchen, we blend the rich flavors of Indian and Pakistani cuisine with the comforting classics of British pub food. Founded by three friends from Hyderabad, Kashmir, and Lahore, we celebrate culinary traditions and cultures through authentic, heartfelt dishes. From aromatic curries to classic fish and chips, our mission is to bring people together over great food.

Our Expertise

At FlavorFog Kitchen, we bring together the rich culinary traditions of India, Pakistan, and British pub cuisine to craft a truly unique dining experience. Our chefs blend their passion and expertise to create dishes that celebrate authenticity, diversity, and exceptional flavor.
Authentic Indian Cuisine

Traditional Pakistani Flavors

British Pub Food
